Biography

Holds a bachelor's and master's degrees from International Ataturk Alatoo University. Before coming to IAAU, N. Shaidullaev worked as Informatics teacher, Republic olympiad and IOI (International Olympiad in Informatics) trainer in Kyrgyz-Turkish Boys High-School named after Ch. Aytmatov. He is one of two founding directors of Research and Development Center of IAAU. Publications "Ruby Basics for Engineering Students", IKECCO'2010 7th International Conference on Electronics and Computer, 2010 "Moodle Methodology and Impact on Students of IAAU" with Sanjar Erdolatov, IKECCO'2011 8th International Conference on Electronics and Computer, 2011
